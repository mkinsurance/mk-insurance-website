# Image Suggestions and Icon Recommendations

## Image Suggestions

- Hero: Indian insurance advisor consulting a family in a bright modern office, with policy folders, tablet, car key and clean blue-green styling.
- Claim assistance: advisor desk with organized claim documents, phone support and calm professional lighting.
- Trust section: muted collage of families, small businesses, travel documents and vehicle ownership moments.
- Avoid: fear-based hospital scenes, cash piles, generic handshake-only stock photos, readable fake policy text and unauthorized insurer logos.

## Logo Guidance

- Replace placeholder insurer logos only with authorized logo files.
- Use optimized WebP or PNG assets under 50 KB where possible.
- Keep partner logos visually restrained so the MK brand remains the primary signal.
